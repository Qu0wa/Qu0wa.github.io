Data Log: The Slime, Mollush

General description

This slime appears to take shape of a white-skinned human female as well as consistantly wearing a Fox Mask. It appears to take a liking to firearms, so much so that she consumes them and molds them from her arm. The projectiles shot from her arms have lower velocity & lack penetrative ability as the bullets from weapons she consumed, but they consume their target slowly. Mollush has not yet been seen without her mask, referred to "Q", being somehwere on her body at all times & appears to converse with Q and treats it as a living being, though it is likely meant to trick onlookers. When donning the mask directly, Mollush undergoes a personality shift when wearing this mask. The only hint of a weakness is the possibility of a core, however no confirmation of a core has been declared.
